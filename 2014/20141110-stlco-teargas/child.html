Tear Gas

CS 40 MM 8 Inch Launchable Projectile
Similar to the 40mm launchable, except this cartridge sends out 5 canisters of tear gas instead of 1. [1]
CS 40 MM Barricade Penetrating Launchable
A projectile that can deliver either liquid or powdered tear gas up to 50 yards. It is designed to go through barriers such as single pane windows, vehicle window and pressed particle wood doors. [1] [2]
CS 40MM Launchable
A projectile that sends out either 1 or 3 canisters of tear gas. [1]
CS 40MM Muzzle Blast
Intended for short range usage, effective only to 30 ft. These blasts are launched and provide quick delivery of tear gas. [1]
CS 40MM Skat Shell Launchable
This projectile has an effective rangle of 60 - 80 yards. After it is fired, it separates into 4-5 sub-munitions that discharge within 30 seconds. Intended for widespread coverage of tear gas. [1]

This projectile is intended for long range usage. It can be launched up to 150 yards. [1]
CS Aerosol Vapor
An aersol container that's typically used indoors. It releases a practically invisible vapor over a span of a few seconds. [1] [2]
CS Continuous Discharge
A grenade that emits tear gas for roughly 35 seconds. It can be launched up to 90 yards and its tear gas can cover up to 300 feet. [1]
CS Hornets Nest Stingball
A grenade that sends out roughly 80 .45 caliber rubber balls with powdered tear gas. [1] [2]
CS Instantaneous Blast
A grenade that instantly emits tear gas and splits at six external groves upon ignition. It is intended for confined areas and close range usage, and can be used in indoor and outdoor situations [1]
CS Launchable Flameless Expulsion
A grenade that sends out tear gas using a CO2 cartridge. The tear gas would affect a confined area of approximately 1500 square feet sent out over a span of 3 seconds. [1]
CS Pocket Tactical
A small grenade (less than 5 inches by 2 inches) that sends out about 25 seconds of tear gas when deployed. [1]
CS Riot Smoke (hand thrown)
A hand thrown grenade that emits tear gas. [1]
CS Smoke Grenade
A grenade that has a burn time of approximately 30 seconds and gives off a dense cloud of teargas. [1]
CS Stingball Grenade
A grenade that explodes sending out roughly 100 rubber balls with tear gas added as an additive powder. [1]
CS Triple Chaser
Three canisters that separate about 20 feet apart when fired. Sends out tear gas for about 30 seconds. [1]

Smoke

White Smoke 40MM Launchable
A projectile that sends out either 1 or 3 canisters of white smoke. [1]
White Smoke Continuous Discharge HC
A grenade that emits white smoke for roughly 35 seconds. It can be launched up to 90 yards and its smoke can cover up to 300 feet. [1]
White Smoke Grenade
Grenade that emits about 30 seconds seconds of white smoke. [1]
White Smoke Grenade Cannister (5210)
Grenade that emits about 20-40 seconds of white smoke. [1]
White Smoke Tactical Grenade (8210)
Grenade that emits about 20-40 seconds of white smoke. A slightly thinner and smaller cannister than the 5210. [1]
