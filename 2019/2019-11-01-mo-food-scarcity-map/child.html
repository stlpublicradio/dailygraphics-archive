St. Louis, southern Missouri counties have highest rates of food insecurity

Feeding America used 2017 census projections and data on poverty to estimate food insecurity in counties nationwide for that year. St. Charles has the lowest rate of food insecurity of any Missouri county, at 9%. St. Louis has the highest, at more than 23%. The estimates show that high rates of food insecurity are more common in rural counties.

Source: Feeding America
