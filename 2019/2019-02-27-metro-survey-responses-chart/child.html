Surveyed riders most often wanted improvements to security presence and activity, rule enforcement

The survey asked people to identify all of their top concerns from the provided list. More than 1,000 respondents said they wanted to see “increased security presence on trains.” Fewer than half of respondents wanted to see that same increase on platforms.

Notes

1,592 people responded to this particular question. About half of those said they used MetroLink at least weekly. People provided multiple answers, so the number of total votes exceeds the number of respondents.

Source: Metro Security Assessment - Rider Survey Summary 2019
