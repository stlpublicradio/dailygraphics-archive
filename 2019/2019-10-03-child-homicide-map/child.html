11 children killed by gun violence in The Ville and Greater Ville since 2004

Dots on the map represent child victims killed by guns since 2004. Red dots are children who were killed by the end of August 2019.

Notes

Locations for homicides reported in parks and near highways are approximate. Police data associates weapon information with an overall crime, not a specific victim, and only lists one weapon if multiple were used. This map omits a 2019 homicide committed with an unverified weapon. St. Louis Public Radio counts children as ages 17 and younger.

Source: St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department
