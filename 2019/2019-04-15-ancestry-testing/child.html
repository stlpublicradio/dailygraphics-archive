How Ancestry Testing Works

Step one:

Individual mails a saliva sample to a DNA ancestry company.

Step two:

Company digitizes that sample, then selects areas of the DNA where variation occurs.

Step three:

Algorithms compare the selection against a huge DNA library to look for patterns. Matches help estimate the ancestry of the person who sent in the sample and find relatives.

Step four:

Individual receives a report on the results.
