Missouri Medicaid enrollment dropped in second half of 2018

Medicaid enrollment in Missouri dropped 7 percent in 2018, the largest one-year drop in a decade. During that year, enrollment in the Medicaid program for children dropped at a rate of 9 percent, slightly steeper than the drop in total enrollment.

Source: Caseload Counter Historical Data, Missouri Department of Social Services
