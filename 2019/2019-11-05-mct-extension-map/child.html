Madison County Trails extension into O’Fallon opens Nov. 16

Madison County Transit has constructed a 7-mile extension to the Goshen Trail, which previously ran from South Roxana at its northern end through Maryville at its southern end. The extension connects to the existing Goshen Trail near I-55 and the Schoolhouse Trail.
