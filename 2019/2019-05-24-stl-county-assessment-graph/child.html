St. Louis County median home values increased by as much as 33%

Early estimates show that median home value increased in nearly all of the 88 St. Louis County municipalities that assessors track. Assessors reported median increases of 23% to 33% for the 10 municipalities that saw the greatest growth. In five municipalities, home value decreased, and in two, it did not change. Median home value also increased in unincorporated St. Louis County.

Notes

Graph shows residential data only and excludes properties that changed use category between 2019 and 2017. Data is preliminary.
