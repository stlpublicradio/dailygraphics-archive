Race days have dropped by 73% since peak in 2000

Fairmont Park Racetrack in 2001 began scheduling fewer race days per year, on average. The track hit its lowest number of race days in 2018. Officials say that with new gambling attractions and renovations, they hope to host 100 days of racing again by 2021.

Source: Illinois Racing Board
