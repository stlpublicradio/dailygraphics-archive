Calorie standards for school meals

School meals must meet minimum and maximum calorie standards by student age group. The regulations come from the U.S. Department of Agriculture, which oversees federal nutrition standards for school meals.

Grade
Breakfast
Lunch
K-5 350-500 cal 550-650 cal
6-8 400-550 cal 600-700 cal
9-12 450-600 cal 750-850 cal

Source: School Nutrition Association
