More than 400 service members die by suicide each year

The Department of Defense has tracked known suicides dating back to 2012. The suicide count peaked in 2018, with 541 deaths. The number of suicides has increased each year since 2014.

Notes

This data does not count retirees who died by suicide.

Source: Department of Defense Quarterly Suicide Report, 1st Quarter, CY 2019
