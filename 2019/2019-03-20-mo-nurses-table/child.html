Nursing vacancy rate in St. Louis region is the highest in Missouri

The Missouri Hospital Association estimates that the vacancy rate for nursing jobs in the state is 13.3 percent. The St. Louis region, at 18 percent, has the highest vacancy rate, and the second-lowest turnover rate among the other regions in Missouri.
