Deaths involving synthetic opioids in St. Louis County spiked in 2016

The number of deaths involving synthetic opioids such as fentanyl have increased every year since at least 2012. That count quadrupled between 2015 and 2016.

Notes

Report collated by St. Louis County using Missouri Department of Health & Senior Services data

Source: St. Louis County Department of Public Health, Drug Overdose Deaths 2018
