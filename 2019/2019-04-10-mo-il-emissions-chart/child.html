Missouri and Illinois carbon dioxide emissions have decreased since 2005

Missouri reported a decrease of 23.5 million metric tons of carbon dioxide emissions since 2005; Illinois reported a decrease of 37.9 during that same time period. Both states ranked among the top 15 emitters of CO2 in 2016, the most recent year data was available.

Notes

Numbers are not adjusted for population.

Source: U.S. Energy Information Administration, Energy-Related Carbon Dioxide Emissions by State, 2005-2016
