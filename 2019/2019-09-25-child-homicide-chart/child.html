Child gun deaths through August are the highest in years in St. Louis

Twelve children died from gun violence in St. Louis this year between January 1 and August 31, according to police data. That’s more than a 300% increase compared to the same eight-month time period last year.

Notes

Data does not depict years prior to 2004 because information provided by the police department did not consistently report cause of death until that year. Police data associates weapon information with an overall crime, not a specific victim, and only lists one weapon if multiple were used. Graphic counts children age 17 and younger.

Source: St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department
