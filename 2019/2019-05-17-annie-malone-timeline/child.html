Annie Malone: A Timeline

1886

The St. Louis Colored Orphans Home located at 1427 N. 12th St. is established.

1905

The orphanage moves to a site on Natural Bridge Avenue.

1910

The first May Day Parade is held.

1922

The orphanage moves to the permanent location 2612 Goode Ave. in the Ville neighborhood.

1924

The home becomes a member agency of United Way of Greater St. Louis.

1946

The St. Louis Colored Orphans Home is renamed the Annie Malone Children's Home.

1980s

Annie Malone Children's Home opens the Boys Group Home and the Family Crisis Center.

1986

Goode Avenue is renamed Annie Malone Drive.

1993

Annie Malone Children's Home becomes Annie Malone Children and Family Services, to reflect the needs of the community.

2005

The parade route for the annual May Day Parade is moved from The Ville to downtown.

2019

The 109th May Day Parade is held in downtown St. Louis.
