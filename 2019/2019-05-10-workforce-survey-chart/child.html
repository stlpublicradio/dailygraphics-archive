Software developers and other tech employees in high demand in St. Louis area

Between June 2017 and May 2018, St. Louis Community College counted more than 200,000 online job postings in the St. Louis metro area. Employers created more than 16,000 postings to seek employees skilled in programming languages or technology. Only registered nurses were in higher demand.

Source: State of the St. Louis Workforce 2018, St. Louis Community College
