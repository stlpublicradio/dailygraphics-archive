Hate crime reports in Missouri decreased in 2018, but so did the number of agencies submitting them

Missouri police agencies reported 39% fewer hate crimes to the FBI in 2018 than in 2017. Of roughly 600 participating agencies, only 22 submitted incident reports last year. Thirty-two submitted reports in 2017.

Source: Federal Bureau of Investigation Uniform Crime Reporting, 2018
