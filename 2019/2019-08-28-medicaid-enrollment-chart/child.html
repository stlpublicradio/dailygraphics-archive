Missouri Medicaid enrollment has declined by 14% since January 2017

Medicaid enrollment in Missouri declined by nearly 138,000 between January 2017 and June 2019. Children account for about 80% of that drop. Other groups enrolled in Medicaid, including elderly and pregnant Missourians, saw smaller declines or marginal increases.

Source: Missouri Department of Social Services
