Replacing Stenger

With Steve Stenger resigning Monday, here’s what happens next for the St. Louis County executive’s office:

  1. County council selects Sam Page, Democratic chairman of the council, to replace Stenger as interim county executive.

  2. County residents will vote on a permanent replacement in a 2020 special election.

  3. The elected official would replace the interim county executive and serve until 2022.
