What is the Board of Freeholders?

A 19-member board that could present voters with a plan to end the so-called “Great Divorce” between St. Louis and St. Louis County. Mayor Lyda Krewson and County Executive Sam Page each appoint nine members. Gov. Mike Parson will pick the final member.

What can it do?

The board is authorized by the state constitution to make changes to governance and service delivery only if city and county voters approve its proposed plan. The board could also suggest keeping things the way they are.
