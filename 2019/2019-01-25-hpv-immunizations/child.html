Teens are vaccinated for HPV less frequently in Missouri than nationwide

In Missouri, males and females aged 13 to 17 often do not receive all recommended doses of the human papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine. Missouri is 43rd in the nation for HPV vaccination rates for females and 41st in the nation for males.

Notes

Data counts only adolescents who received the full recommended dose of HPV vaccine for their age group.

Source: CDC, National Immunization Survey-Teen
