Hispanic-owned businesses in St. Charles County more than doubled between 2007 and 2012

St. Charles County had about 220 Hispanic-owned businesses in 2007, according to a U.S. Census Bureau survey. Five years later, that number rose to more than 500, a 160% increase. St. Louis County also added about 300 Hispanic-owned businesses during that period but the county already had about 1,000 of them in 2007, accounting for a 27% increase.

Notes

Data excludes business owners reported as equally Hispanic and non-Hispanic.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau Survey of Business Owners 2007, 2012
