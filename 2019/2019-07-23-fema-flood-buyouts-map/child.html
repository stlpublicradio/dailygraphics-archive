Only properties bought out by FEMA since 2002 are subject to federal monitoring

In 2002, FEMA began requiring local governments to produce reports that detail how buyout properties are used. In the St. Louis region, 536 properties are subject to monitoring. That accounts for just 10% of those bought out between 1990 and 2016.
