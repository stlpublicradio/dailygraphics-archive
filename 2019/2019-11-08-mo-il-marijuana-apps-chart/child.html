Early medical card approvals in Missouri eclipse those in Illinois

Illinois opened medical card applications and approved not quite 8,000 in about two years. Missouri’s applications have been open for just a few months, but the state has already approved more than 17,000 medical card users.

Notes

The first available medical card application count for Illinois is in July 2015, 10 months after the program started. Missouri data reflects the number of medical cards approved as of Nov. 7, 2019.

Source: United States Census Bureau; Missouri Department of Health and Senior Services; Illinois Department of Public Health
