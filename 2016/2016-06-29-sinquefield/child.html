Rex Sinquefield’s committees

The retired financier has set up organizations to distribute money to candidates. Each organization has a different focus: tax cuts, crime, etc. Spokesman Travis Brown said Sinquefield wants attention on those things, not on himself: “The primary season is more about issue-based elections, not a donor-based election. We don’t want the message to be about a donor, we want it to be about the issues.”
