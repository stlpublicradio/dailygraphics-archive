Registered voters vs. population

Many St. Louis County municipalities reported more registered voters for 2010 and 2014 than residents of voting age according to the 2010 census. This chart shows the registered voters as a percentage of voting-age population: Values of more than 100 percent mean more registered voters than adults.

Source: U.S. Census; St. Louis County voter rolls
