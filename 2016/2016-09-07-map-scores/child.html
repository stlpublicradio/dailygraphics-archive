Missouri Assessment Program: 2016 results

Fewer than half of Missouri students in grades five through eight scored proficient or advanced in math in 2016. Scores in English were around 60 percent.

Super Subgroup Math: Proficient or Advanced

The super subgroup includes students in at least one of the following categories: Black, Hispanic, students with disabilities, English language learners, low income students.
