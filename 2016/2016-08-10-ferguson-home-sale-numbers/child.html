Percent change in home sales (year over year)

The chart shows the percent change in home sales from the same quarter in the previous year. In the last quarter of 2014 and first quarter of 2015, sales were down about 40% in Ferguson compared to 2013-2014; in the first two quarters of 2016, sales were up more than 50% compared to 2015.

Source: St. Louis Realtors
