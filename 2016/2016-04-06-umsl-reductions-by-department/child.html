Where UMSL may make its cuts (FY17-18)

UMSL plans on $12 million in reductions over two years, with an estimated 85 full-time-equivalent positions being eliminated. The chart shows reductions over two years by department, and how much of the planned cuts will come from compensation, activities, or other.

Notes

Grad School: Other represents $400,000 reduction in graduate remission assistantships
Optometry: Other represents transfers to plant fund
Budget Development & Planning: Other represents reduction in bad debt
