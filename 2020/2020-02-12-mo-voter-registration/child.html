Early voter registrations shot up in St. Charles County for 2020 presidential primary

The election board counts new voters from January until registration for primaries closes in mid-February. In the past five years, relatively few new voters have registered for spring primary elections in St. Charles County. But in 2019 and 2020, registrations increased drastically. Officials attribute the increase in part to Missouri’s online registration system.

Notes

Data is not year-round. Data reflects new voter registrations that occurred from Jan. 1 to Feb. 11 each year.

Source: St. Charles County Election Authority
