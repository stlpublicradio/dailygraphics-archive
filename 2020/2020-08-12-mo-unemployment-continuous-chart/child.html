More than 170,000 Missourians requested unemployment in early August

Unemployment applications in Missouri spiked in March, as cities throughout the state and around the nation began restricting business operations to slow the spread of coronavirus. Many people returned to work as businesses reopened. But the number on unemployment insurance in the beginning of August was still more than five times higher than in January.

Source: U.S. Employment and Training Administration; State Unemployment Insurance Weekly Claims Report - August 2020
