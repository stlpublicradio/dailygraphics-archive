Hard-to-count census areas concentrated in north, southeast St. Louis

The Census Bureau expects to need extra resources to count all of the residents in some St. Louis communities. The bureau uses low response scores to estimate how hard it will be to reach people in a given area based on measures like how reliably they responded to mail during past census counts.

Source: 2019 Planning Database, U.S. Census Bureau
