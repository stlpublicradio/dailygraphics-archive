Applications for unemployment in Missouri increased by more than 10 times

In the second week of March, people filed around 4,000 applications for unemployment benefits to the Missouri Department of Labor. That number increased drastically the following week, as coronavirus-related closures hit businesses. More than 42,000 people filed claims between March 14 and March 21.

Notes

Data shows the department’s initial claims from 12/14/2019 through 3/21/2020. Older data is incomplete.

Source: Department of Labor and Industrial Relations
