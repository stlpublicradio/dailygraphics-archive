Granite City crime-free housing violations

Granite City told landlords to kick out tenants on more than 300 occasions for crime-free housing violations between 2014 and 2018.

Who was accused?

Tenant(s) were accused of wrongdoing in the majority of the crime-free housing violations requiring an eviction, but some renters also faced eviction because of allegations against guest(s).

Who was convicted?

Many of the people accused of wrongdoing were never convicted of a crime.

Notes

In one instance, both a guest and another person, but not a tenant, were accused of a crime. This number amounts to less than 1%.
