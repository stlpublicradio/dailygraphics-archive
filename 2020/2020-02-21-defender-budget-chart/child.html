State funding lags behind Missouri public defender budget requests

The Missouri State Public Defender’s office has requested more funding from the state each fiscal year since 2016, but the legislature has only incrementally increased the office’s budget. In fiscal year 2020, the department received $51.6 million, about $28 million, or 35%, less than it requested.
