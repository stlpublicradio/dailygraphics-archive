The Census Calendar

March 12-20, 2020

Renters and homeowners will receive a letter in the mailbox. It will explain how to complete the census form.

March 30 - April 1, 2020

Census workers will count homeless individuals.

April 1, 2020

People will be asked to report how many people lived in their home on this day. Census workers will also canvas people in communal living situations, like college students and seniors in retirement homes.

May - June, 2020

Census workers will visit the addresses of people who did not respond to the questionnaire.

March 31, 2021

The official new population count is sent to each state. Lawmakers can now redraw legislative districts.

Source: AP
