Indian community growth outpaces Mexican-born population in the St. Louis region

In 2010, the largest number of St. Louis-area residents born outside the U.S. were Mexico natives. But by 2018, the U.S. Census Bureau estimated that the region’s Indian-born population exceeded its Mexican-born population.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau, ACS 2010 and 2018 5-Year Estimates
