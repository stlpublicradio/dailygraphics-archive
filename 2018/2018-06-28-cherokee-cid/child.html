Vacant properties surveyed near the Cherokee Street Community Improvement District since 2017

St. Louis building inspectors reported 389 properties as vacant in the Benton Park, Benton Park West, Gravois Park and Marine Villa neighborhoods from January 1, 2017 through June 19, 2018. On average, vacancies have increased slightly in the neighborhoods since 2008.

Notes

Boundaries are approximate. City officials noted that some data may not be up to date regarding vacant properties.

Source: St. Louis City Ordinances; St. Louis Building Division
