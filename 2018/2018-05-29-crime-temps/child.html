Temperatures and violent crime

Over the last decade, violent crime in St. Louis has risen with the temperature — to a point. Since 2008, there have been about 16.5 violent crimes in the city per day on average. During the spring and summer months, there were about 17.5 per day on average.

Notes

Data for March-August, 2008-2018. “Violent crimes” include those coded in the UCR Part I categories of homicide, rape, robbery and aggravated assault.

Source: St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department; NOAA
