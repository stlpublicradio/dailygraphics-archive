Northside Regeneration TIF

The Northside Regeneration TIF stretches from I-64/40 to Natural Bridge Avenue, and from Grand Boulevard on the west to the Stan Musial Veterans Memorial Bridge interchange on the east. Paul McKee's Northside Regeneration project does not own all land within the TIF district.

Notes

Boundaries are approximate.
