Missouri budget appropriations and income, FY 2018

The governor’s recommended operating budget sends 35 percent of appropriations from general revenue — about $3.3 billion — to elementary and secondary education. The total appropriations are about $9.4 billion.

Income

About 71 percent of Missouri’s $9.4 billion estimated revenue is from the individual income tax. That’s about $6.6 billion. The state will get about $275 million from the corporate tax.

Source: Missouri Office of Administration
