Charter schools find homes in former parochial, commercial buildings

Charter schools have struggled to locate sites that can host schools and expand their facilities. A survey of existing charter schools shows that the majority are in former church-run schools, or industrial spaces such as warehouses. A few schools are in renovated St. Louis Public Schools facilities.
