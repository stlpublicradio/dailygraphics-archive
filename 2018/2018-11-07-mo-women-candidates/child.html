Missouri statehouse elections see more women win in 2018

Of the 95 women who competed for seats in the Missouri Senate and House of Representatives on Tuesday, 28 were incumbents. All of those 28 won their races, and another 18 non-incumbents took seats this year. In total, five more women won statehouse seats in 2018 than in 2016.

Notes

Numbers do not reflect incumbent women candidates who lost re-election.

Source: The Sue Shear Institute for Women in Public Life
