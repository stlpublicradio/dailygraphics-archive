City saw fewer opioid deaths in 2017, St. Louis and St. Charles counties saw more

According to preliminary 2017 numbers, opioid deaths are up across the region. St. Louis, where deaths more than doubled between 2015 and 2016, saw a 10 percent decrease last year. There were about 23 more deaths in St. Louis County and 26 more in St. Charles County in 2017.

Source: National Council on Alcoholism and Drug Abuse, St. Louis
