People to Know

Prosecution

Kim Gardner

Circuit Attorney

City of St. Louis
  • Former Democratic state representative from the 77th District
  • First African-American St. Louis circuit attorney

Robert Dierker

Chief Trial Assistant

City of St. Louis
  • Former St. Louis circuit judge
  • In 2010, struck down Paul McKee’s proposed development in north St. Louis. The Missouri Supreme Court later said McKee’s Northside Regeneration Initiative could go ahead.

Robert Steele

First Assistant Circuit Attorney

City of St. Louis
  • Served as the lead attorney handling death penalty cases for the St. Louis office of the public defender
  • Led the prosecution of former St. Louis Metropolitan Police officer Jason Stockley for the 2011 death of Anthony Lamar Smith. A judge found Stockley not guilty in September.

Ronald Sullivan

Director of Criminal Justice Institute

Harvard University Law School
  • Helped represent the family of Michael Brown in their wrongful death lawsuit against the city of Ferguson
  • Was a member of the defense team for former New England Patriots tight end Aaron Hernandez
