More women running for Missouri legislature in 2018 than any year since at least 1992

There will be nearly 100 women on the ballot for the Missouri legislature this year. In 2002, the next highest year, 74 women ran for office. The most seats won by women in a year is 43, in 2000.

Source: Missouri Secretary of State’s office; 2018 data from The Sue Shear Institute for Women in Public Life
