Veteran unemployment rate has declined since 2010

The unemployment rate for veterans has been cut in half since 2010. Those who served in the military after September 2001 (Gulf War Era II) continue to have the highest unemployment, at 4.5 percent. Veterans who served during the Gulf War prior to September 2001, as well as those not classified in other wartime periods, have the lowest rate, at 3.1 percent.

Notes

Veterans who served during the periods indicated are included, regardless of direct participation in the conflicts.

Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics
