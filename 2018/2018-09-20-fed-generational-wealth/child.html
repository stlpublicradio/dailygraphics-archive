Younger families have lower net worths than predicted

A St. Louis Federal Reserve paper found that families with heads of households born between 1980 and 1989 had a median net worth 34 percent lower than predicted in 2016. The groups with heads of households born between 1960 and 1969, and 1970 and 1979 also had median net worths less than predicted.

Notes

Net worth predictions were made for 1989, 2007, 2010, 2013 and 2016.

Source: The Demographics of Wealth: How Education, Race and Birth Year Shape Financial Outcomes, Essay No. 2. Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
