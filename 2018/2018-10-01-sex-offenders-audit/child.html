Across Missouri, nearly 8 percent of sex offenders are non-compliant with registration requirements

A report from Missouri Auditor Nicole Galloway found that more than 1,200 sex offenders in Missouri failed to meet requirements for registration. Butler, Jackson and Stoddard counties all had registration issues with 20 percent or more offenders.

Notes

Data accessed on May 23, 2018

Source: Missouri State Auditor's office
