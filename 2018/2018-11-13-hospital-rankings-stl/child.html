Majority of St. Louis–area hospitals rated below "A" for safety

Fewer than 20 percent of hospitals received top safety ratings in the St. Louis metro region. Two A-rated hospitals were in Missouri; three were in Metro East. The rest received B, C and D grades, indicating potential problems with safety practices or patient outcomes.
