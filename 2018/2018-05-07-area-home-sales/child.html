Average home sale price rising faster in Kirkwood than county average

Between 2013 and 2017, the mean home sale price in Kirkwood rose from about $289,000 to about $382,000 — about 32 percent. Over the same period, the mean price in St. Louis County rose from about $216,000 to about $255,000, or about 18 percent.

Notes

Average price is the mean, not the median.

Source: MARIS
