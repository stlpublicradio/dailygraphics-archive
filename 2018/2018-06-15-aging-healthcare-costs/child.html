Missouri’s costs for nursing homes, assisted living are below national average

The median cost for a home health aide in Missouri was about in line with the national median, but costs for both assisted living and nursing homes were about 30 percent below the national median.

Notes

Nursing home and assisted living facility costs are based on private room rates; home health aide costs are based on 44-hour work weeks.

Source: Genworth 2017 Cost of Care Survey, conducted by CareScout, June 2017
