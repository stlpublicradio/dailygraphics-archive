Demand outpaces supply for free summer meals

No Kid Hungry Missouri calculated how many meals would need to be served to feed every free or reduced-price eligible child twice a day for 50 days over the summer. The percent of coverage is the number of meals actually served in summer 2017 compared to how many were needed to meet that twice-daily goal.
