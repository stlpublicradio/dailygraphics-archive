Occupancy decreased in most Missouri tourist destinations between this and last year

Hotels measure occupancy percentages by comparing the number of beds available to the number of beds sold. Statewide, the occupancy percentage decreased about 2 percent in 2018 between January and August compared to the same time period in 2017.

Notes

Numbers reflect year-to-date occupancy rates for Jan. - Aug. of each year. All results are based on a sample of hotels. Stats for Kansas City and St. Louis numbers include occupancy in hotels located outside of Missouri in their greater metro areas.

Source: Missouri Hotel & Lodging Association STR Destination Report, Sep. 18, 2018
