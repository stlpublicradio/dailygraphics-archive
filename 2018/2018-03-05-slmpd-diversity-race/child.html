Black officers make up about a third of the city’s police department

The racial makeup of the St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department has changed little since 2008. In the city, black and white residents each make up about 47 percent of the population, with about 7 percent of residents identifying as another race.

Percentages may not add to 100 due to rounding.
