St. Louis drops out of top 20 largest metropolitan areas

Between 2010 and 2017, the St. Louis metropolitan area grew by an estimated 17,000 people. But with around 2.8 million people in 2017, it was passed over by Denver and Baltimore to drop to the 21st largest in the country.
