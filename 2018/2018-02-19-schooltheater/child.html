How Two High School Drama Programs Pay for Plays

Clayton’s theater program receives a sizable amount of funding from its school district. But McCluer’s theater program receives less from its district and also relies on fundraising from students. Students at both schools sell tickets. The city of Clayton’s median household income is more than twice that of Ferguson’s, where many McCluer students live.
