Patients admitted yearly to St. Louis Children’s Hospital with gun-related injuries has increased since 2011

In the metro region, St. Louis Children’s Hospital sees one of the highest rates of gun-related injuries in child patients. The majority of patients with gun-related injuries were older than 12.

Notes

Data for 2018 tracks January 1 through September 21.

Source: St. Louis Children’s Hospital
