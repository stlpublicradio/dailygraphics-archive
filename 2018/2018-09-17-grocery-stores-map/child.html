19 Shop 'n Save Locations to be Rebranded as Schnucks Stores

Parent company SuperValu has sold 19 Shop 'n Save groceries to Schnucks Markets. SuperValu is looking for buyers for the remaining 17 locations, which will close by the end of the year unless purchased. Schnucks representatives say they purchased in stores that lack Schnucks Markets or have successful stores already.
