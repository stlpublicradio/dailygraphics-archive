How long does it take to reach pay equity?

The median pay for white men who worked full time, year-round in 2016 was $61,197. Every other racial and ethnic group except Asian men earned less. How much less? Median salaries for other groups ranged from $51,381 for Asian women to $32,037 for Hispanic women. That median salary for Hispanic women works out to an hourly wage of $15.34. At that rate, it would take them an extra 238 working days — almost another full working year — to earn the median yearly salary for white men.

The median wage for Asian men — $32.20 per hour — is higher than the median white male salary. So they earn the same amount as the median white male 23 days faster.
