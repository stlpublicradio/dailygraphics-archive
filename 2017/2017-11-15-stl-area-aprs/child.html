St. Louis-area district Annual Performance Reports

Each chart shows a local district’s APR score, in percentage of possible points earned, over the past four years. The gray line represents the average of all these districts. Between 2016 and 2017, Lift For Life’s score increased by nearly 18 percentage points. Sunrise, Pattonville and Orchard Farm each earned 100 percent of their possible points for the first time since 2014.

Source: Missouri Department of Elementary and Secondary Education
