Prop 2 failed by 3,240 votes

About 53% of voters said "No" to Proposition 2, which would have funded a stadium for a MLS team. The proposition got more than 50% support in eight of the city's 28 wards. Its highest margin was in the 16th Ward, where 59% of voters approved.

Notes

Results are unofficial.

Source: St. Louis Board of Election Commissioners
