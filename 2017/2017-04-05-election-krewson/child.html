Lyda Krewson won with 68 percent of the vote

Lyda Krewson will be the first woman to be mayor of St. Louis, winning by nearly 30,000 votes. She received fewer than 50 percent of the votes cast in three of the city's 28 wards. Her strongest support was in Ward 28, where she won more than 83 percent of the votes cast.

Notes

Results are unofficial.

Source: St. Louis Board of Election Commissioners
