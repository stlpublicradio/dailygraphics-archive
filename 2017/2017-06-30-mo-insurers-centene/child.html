Centene will fill the gap when Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Kansas City leaves

In Missouri, HealthCare.gov offers plans from four insurers: Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Kansas City (BCBS-KC), Anthem, Cigna and Humana. Centene announced it will enter the market in 40 counties and BCBS-KC announced it will leave.

Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Kansas City announced it was leaving

Before Centene’s announcement, BCBS-KC said it was pulling out of Missouri, leaving many counties in northwest Missouri with no insurers.

But Centene will fill the gap

The 40 counties that Centene plans to enter cover BCBS-KC’s exit, meaning all counties will still have at least one insurer.
