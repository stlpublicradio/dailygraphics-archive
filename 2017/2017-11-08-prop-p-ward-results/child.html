Prop P passed by almost 8,000 votes

Prop P, a sales tax increase with the money going toward public safety, passed with nearly 60 percent of St. Louis voters' approval. Just over 40,000 voters turned out across the city, a turnout of about 21 percent.

Notes

Results are unofficial.

Source: St. Louis Board of Election Commissioners
