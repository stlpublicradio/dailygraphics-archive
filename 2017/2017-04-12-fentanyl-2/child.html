Fentanyl-related deaths on the rise

In St. Louis and St. Louis County, fentanyl-related deaths accounted for nearly half of all overdose deaths in 2016. They were less than a quarter of overdose deaths in St. Louis and St. Louis County in 2015.

Notes

*Total overdose deaths were not available
