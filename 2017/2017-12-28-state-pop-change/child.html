Missouri grows, Illinois shrinks

From July 2016 to July 2017, Missouri grew by about 22,000 people, while Illinois lost nearly 34,000. Illinois outpaced Missouri in natural increase, netting nearly 50,000 new residents when taking into account only births and deaths. Missouri gained just over 15,000 by that measure. However, Illinois lost more than 81,000 people to migration, when Missouri saw an influx of over 7,000 people.

Source: U.S. Census
