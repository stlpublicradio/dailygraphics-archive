Wards with higher turnout had stronger support for Prop P — mostly

The 12th, 16th and 23rd wards had the highest turnout in Tuesday’s election, and voters in those wards also showed the strongest support for Prop P — more than 70 percent of voters in each ward said “yes” to the sales tax increase to fund public safety.

Notes

Results are unofficial.

Source: St. Louis Board of Election Commissioners
