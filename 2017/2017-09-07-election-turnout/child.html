St. Charles County Republicans were region’s third-largest voting bloc in 2016

After Democrats and Republicans in St. Louis County, Republicans in St. Charles County made up the largest voting bloc by party in the region in 2016. It was the first year they’ve turned out more than Democrats in St. Louis. In the 2012 presidential election, more voters in St. Louis cast ballots for Barack Obama and Joe Biden than voters in St. Charles County did for Mitt Romney and Paul Ryan.

Source: Open Elections and Missouri Secretary of State
