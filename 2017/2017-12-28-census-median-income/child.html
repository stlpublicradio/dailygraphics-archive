Median incomes up for white St. Louisans since 2005, down slightly for everyone else

After adjusting for inflation, the median income of white city residents has gone up by about $10,000 since 2005. Despite increases in recent years, black residents’ median income fell by about $1,000 over the entire period. In the metro area, median income for both black and white residents has fallen slightly.

Notes

All figures are in 2016 dollars.

Source: American Community Survey, 2005-2016 1-year estimates
