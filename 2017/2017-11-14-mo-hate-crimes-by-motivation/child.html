FBI counted fewer hate crimes in Missouri in 2016

According to the FBI, hate crimes reported in Missouri dropped from 100 in 2015 to 88 in 2016. Reported crimes motivated by a person’s race or ethnicity were slightly down. But there were more crimes motivated by sexual orientation and gender.

Notes

Numbers include one incident reported with more than one motivation.

Source: Federal Bureau of Investigation hate crime statistics 2015-2016
