How education correlates with poverty in St. Louis

In 2016, around 40 percent of people in St. Louis who didn’t finish high school lived in poverty. That number was cut in half for those who attended any college. It drops below 10 percent for those with at least a bachelor’s degree.

Notes

Lighter bars indicate margin of error.

Source: American Community Survey, 2016 1-year estimates
