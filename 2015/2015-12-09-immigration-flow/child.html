Refugees resettled in Missouri

More than 12,000 refugees from 41 different countries have resettled in Missouri over the past decade. The diagram below shows the top 5 countries refugees have come from, and where in the region they were initially sent. However, many refugee families do not stay in the same town after their initial resettlement, and move to find work or live near larger communities that share their language. “Secondary migration” would not be reflected in this data set.

Source: U.S. Dept. of State
