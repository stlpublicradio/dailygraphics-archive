Getting to Fair St. Louis

Although they lowered the price for parking in Forest Park from $30 to $20, Fair St. Louis officials still say they encourage people planning to attend to use alternate forms of transportation. Here's how to get to the fair:

 
 

Metrolink

Passengers from Illinois or using the Blue Line in Missouri should board and exit at Skinker; passengers using the Red Line in Missouri should board and exit at Forest Park.

 
 

Bike

Bike valets will be available at the intersection of Lagoon Drive and Skinker and at Gate 2. Bike self-parking will be available at Gate 4

 
 

Shuttle

Free bus shuttles will circulate around the east side of Forest Park. Fee-based golf cart shuttles will also be available.

 
 

Park

Parking in Forest Park (marked lots only) and at Washington University's lot at Skinker is $20. Free parking is available on the west side of Washington University's campus, at St. Louis Community College - Forest Park, and in Central West End lots at Newstead and Clayton/Duncan. Vehicle entrance to the park is via marked entrances only, at Union/Lindell and Hampton.
