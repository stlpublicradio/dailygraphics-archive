School to Prison Pipeline: A Quiz

Two longitudinal studies in the past four years have looked at the issue of school suspension as it relates to interactions with the juvenile / criminal justice system - one in Texas and one in Pittsburgh. Both yield similar results, although the Texas study included a much larger sample of students (about 1,000,000 in Texas and just over 1,000 in Pittsburgh). The first five questions below are drawn from the Texas study titled, "Breaking School Rules."
