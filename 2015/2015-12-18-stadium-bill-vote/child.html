Board Bill 219

The bill to fund a proposed riverfront football stadium was approved 17-10, the same total as Wednesday’s preliminary vote. The only changes: Larry Arnowitz, who voted Yes on Wednesday, abstained today and Beth Murphy, who was absent on Wednesday, voted Yes today.
