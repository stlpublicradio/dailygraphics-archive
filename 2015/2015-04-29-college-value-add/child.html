Value-added rankings of some area colleges

Brookings ranked colleges using a metric they call "value-added". This metric attempts to determine how much better or worse a graduate from a specific college does than a graduate with similar characteristics attending the same type of institution in a similar location.

Brookings looked at several characteristics and scored colleges from 1-100 with 100 being the top score. We've highlighted three characteristics below.

  • Mid-career salary: Represents the median mid-career salary of graduates.
  • Loan Payback: Represents federal loan repayment rates.
  • Earning Potential: Represents the average salary of all alumni's occupations.

Notes

* Two-year colleges
