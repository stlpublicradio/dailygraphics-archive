Road Closed

Kingshighway will close between Shaw and Vandeventer on July 6.

Detours:

  • Southbound drivers will turn left onto Shaw, then right onto Vandeventer, then a slight left where Vandeventer meets Kingshighway.
  • Northbound drivers will make a right onto Vandeventer and continue north, crossing under I-44 before making a left onto McCree and then a right on Kingshighway.
  • Drivers on eastbound I-44 who want to take Kingshighway south should exit at Vandeventer and turn right.
