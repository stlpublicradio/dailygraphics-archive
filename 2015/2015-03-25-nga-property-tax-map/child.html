Northside Regeneration and the NGA footprint

Northside Regeneration LLC owns 358 parcels (shown in red/yellow) of the 554 inside the area the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency is considering relocating to in north St. Louis. The company paid property taxes on one of the parcels in 2013 and 2014: 1516 N. Jefferson. The rest of the parcels were unpaid, totaling $40,639.05.

Source: Cass & Jefferson Redevelopment Area Blight Analysis; Taxes owed compiled from St. Louis' Address Lookup
