Charter Schools MAP scores (percent proficient or advanced)

City Garden Montessori, North Side Community School and Gateway Science Academy all scored better than the Missouri average in both English and math. Those schools plus Lafayette Preparatory Academy, Premier Charter School, KIPP St. Louis, St. Louis Language Immersion Schools and EAGLE College Prep all did better than the St. Louis Public Schools average.

Source: Missouri Department of Elementary and Secondary Education
