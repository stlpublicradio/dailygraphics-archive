Who's out in Ferguson?

Since the shooting death of Michael Brown on Aug. 9, 2014, several Ferguson city employees have left their jobs:

Nov. 26, 2014: Police Officer Darren Wilson resigns

March 4: Court Clerk Mary Ann Twitty was fired

March 5: Police Officers Rick Henke and William Mudd resign

March 9: Municipal Judge Ron Brockmeyer resigns

March 10: City Manager John Shaw resigns

March 11: Police Chief Thomas Jackson resigns
