Heroin treatment in St. Louis

There are around 500 beds available for inpatient treatment, while organizations can take about 3,000 more people for outpatient treatment.

We compiled this data through interviews, surveys and public information. Because not every agency responded to our requests for information, it's an incomplete picture of treatment options in the region.

Centers with green background have state funding and accept patients who are unable to pay.
